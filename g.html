Racecraft and Online / Multiplayer Tips

  1. Clean driving = better rating

    • ACC has driver rating & safety rating. Crashes, cutting track, aggressive blocking will harm these. Clean, consistent driving gets you into better lobbies, higher quality races. Traxion.GG+1

    • Be predictable on track — avoid sudden moves, leave space when being overtaken. Player Assist+1

  2. Race starts & formation laps

    • During formation laps, warm up tyres and brakes via light braking/throttle, weaving etc. So you’re in better shape when the race begins. Traxion.GG

    • Choose your side for turn 1 carefully; outside line may give a bit more space if things get messy. Risk vs reward matters. Traxion.GG

  3. Mindset: consistency over ultimate lap time

    • Lap after lap, hitting the same braking / turn‑in / exit points builds up performance. One “mistake” can cost more than several perfect corners combined. Sim Racing Setup+2Spottis+2

    • Focus on what YOU can control: lines, braking, unforced errors.

  4. Practice in varied conditions

    • Rain, night, track temp changes: all affect grip and behaviour. Being good in one condition doesn’t mean you’ll be ok everywhere. Spottis+1

    • Also practice qualifying scenarios (low fuel, fresh tyres) versus race scenarios (tyre wear, fuel load).
