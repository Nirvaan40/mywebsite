About us

 

Welcome to Assetto Corsa Competizione tips., your go‑to spot for beginner-friendly tips, tricks, and setups in Assetto Corsa Competizione (ACC)!

Whether you’re just starting out or looking to sharpen your racing skills, we’re here to help you get the most out of your virtual racing experience. We focus on practical advice—like choosing the right tracks, mastering your car, and tuning setups that balance performance and ease of use.

Our goal is to make the complex world of sim racing more accessible, fun, and rewarding. We believe every driver can improve with the right guidance, so we keep our content straightforward, tested, and easy to understand.

Why Choose Us?

  • Beginner Friendly: We specialize in clear tips for newcomers without the overwhelming jargon.

  • Track & Car Focus: Detailed guides on beginner-friendly tracks like Monza, paired with setups for popular cars like the BMW M4 GT3.

  • Community Driven: We listen, adapt, and update our content based on real racer feedback.

  • Constant Updates: We keep an eye on new patches, cars, and tracks to keep you ahead of the curve.
